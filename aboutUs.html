About Us

ShenZhen Janue Life Co., LTD was established in 2012, specialized in aroma diffusers, nebulizing diffusers, car aromatherapy diffusers, usb aroma diffusers and oral irrigators. Janue Life is a modern high-tech enterprise with integration of scientific research and development manufacturer is one of the well-known manufacturers, enjoy the reputation of the South China ultrasonic aroma diffuser family .

With strong R&D capability we have equipped with 5000 square meters workshop,100 employees and5 assembly lines.Annual capacity is more than 20,000,000 pieces. We have strictly executed ISO9001:2000 system in the whole process of its production, ranging from inspection of raw materials to monitoring of production process.Adopting material with non-toxic,all products passed EMC,FCC,CCC, GS, UL, ROSH. Products are exported to United States, Japan, South Korea Australia, Belgium, France，other Europe country and China .

Enterprise has been committed to its motto in production and service:" quality supreme, customers first, excellence pursuance" while it explores constantly to excel and to perfect its management.Greatly enhance our products at home and abroad market share. 5 years of unremitting efforts, the rapid development of the company, with a complete and scientific quality management system, our integrity, strength and quality of products have recognized by customers. Adhering to the "professional, integrity, innovation, quality, Thanksgiving," the spirit of enterprise, to create oral irrigator & aroma diffuser leading fashion brand, has become China's largest oral irrigator & aroma products industry occupies an important position.

Janue's brand was inspired by a belief in a contemporary and modern lifestyle complemented by traditional Chinese crafts, philosophy and aesthetics .we spread the traditional Chinese cultural values of affinity with and respect for nature and through this balance of body, mind and soul by returning to a life of purity and simplicity. Our product series support and generate this unique lifestyle to result in a better, simpler, and higher quality living environment
